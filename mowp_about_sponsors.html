Our Community

Meals on Wheels extends sincere gratitude to our valued donors. Thank you for being indispensable partners in our mission to ensure no senior goes hungry or feels forgotten.

Community Partners
Publix
Driven by a commitment to mutual care, Publix significantly supports hunger relief in the Zephyrhills community year-round through the Good Together food donation program and Publix Super Markets Charities, directly benefiting homebound seniors and vulnerable populations.
Sam's Club
The Sam's Club Retail Store Donation Program recovers and donates high-quality surplus food channeled to Feeding Tampa Bay, which distributes it to local pantries and programs like East Pasco Meals on Wheels.
Costco Wholesale
The nearest Costco warehouse donates surplus, high-quality perishables to Feeding Tampa Bay, which distributes them to local pantries and organizations like Meals on Wheels.
Wawa
The Wawa Share program ensures unsold, wholesome food from Zephyrhills stores is regularly donated directly to a local food pantry or hunger relief partner.
Chick-fil-A
Through the Chick-fil-A Shared Table® program, surplus food is donated to local non-profits. Their opening also included a $25,000 donation to Feeding America distributed to Feeding Tampa Bay.
Starbucks
Starbucks' FoodShare program rescues unsold, ready-to-eat food daily from local stores, collected by refrigerated transport and delivered to Feeding Tampa Bay for distribution in the Zephyrhills community.
Dollar General
Local DG stores contribute in-kind food donations to Feeding Tampa Bay, providing meals to food-insecure neighbors while offering residents affordable access to essential grocery items.
Winn-Dixie
The Zephyrhills Winn-Dixie directly donates fresh produce, meat, dairy, and other goods to Feeding Tampa Bay, which distributes essential supplies to local food pantries and hunger relief agencies.
Target
Target stores contribute resources and high-quality goods to regional food networks, offering critical backing for vital services like Meals on Wheels and directly improving the lives of homebound seniors.
Longhorn Steakhouse
Through local outreach and food donation initiatives, Longhorn's dedication to corporate responsibility offers essential backing for critical programs like Meals on Wheels, directly enhancing the well-being of homebound seniors.
