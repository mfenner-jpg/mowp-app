About Us
Proudly serving East Pasco since 1974

Who We Are

Meals on Wheels of Pasco County has served as an essential lifeline since 1974. As a community-funded nonprofit, our mission is simple: ensure no senior, person with a disability, or homebound resident faces hunger or social isolation.

Our Mission

We deliver not just food, but independence and care. Through our core meal delivery, the Feeding You With Love Food Pantry, and The Genesis Center for social engagement, we are committed to comprehensive care across Pasco County.

Community Funded

Unlike many programs, Meals on Wheels Pasco is supported primarily by local gifts, fundraisers, and grants — not government funding. Truly an organization of the community, for the community.
