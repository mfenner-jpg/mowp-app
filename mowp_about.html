What We Do

For decades, Meals on Wheels Pasco has been a beacon of hope for our homebound neighbors. Founded in 1974, we ensure that no senior, person with a disability, or homebound community member goes hungry or feels isolated.

Powered by Our Community

Unlike many programs, we are proudly supported by local gifts, fundraisers, and grants — not government funding. This makes us an organization of the community, for the community.

Our Core Programs

We broaden our impact through the Feeding You With Love Food Pantry, The Genesis Center for social connection, and our partnership with CHIPZ to serve the most vulnerable in Zephyrhills.
